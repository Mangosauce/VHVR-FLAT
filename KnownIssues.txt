Known Issues - VHVR FlatVR Alpha 0.1.0

Active:
1.  Main menu UI becomes zoomed/corrupted after logout. May appear zoomed in or cursor may not function properly. Recommended restart of game or use keyboard keys to navigate into game.
2. Giant helmet rendering bug when transitioning from first person to third person. If It happens, de-equip and re-equip helmet.
3. HUD hide controls do not fully work in FlatVR.
4. Mouse Y-axis aiming is not implemented.
5. Some movement/orientation state may need manual VHVR recentering after mode switches with the Home hotkey.

Recently resolved:
- Headset crosshair restoration after VRC to FlatVR.
- Hover text restoration after VRC to FlatVR.
- Controller cursor cleanup after VRC to FlatVR.
- Crosshair depth/flicker caused by local player self-hit.

Recommended:
Back up your original ValheimVRMod.dll before installing.
